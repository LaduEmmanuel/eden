Welcome to eden primary school

Eden primary School opened its gates to the world in 2008. In marked 16 years of existence this year. It has made its mark in the education history of the country as a Towering Academic Giant. It is located in Koboko district. It is a mixed Boarding primary School, It has two campuses; Main Campus in Triangle ward, Dembelenga village on Koboko-Uraba Highway 2 Km after koboko town and Eden junior Campus 3km along koboko-Yumbe road. All our campuses have uniform policy and guidelines as we aim to pass out a uniform product. The schools boast of a clean environment, state of the art facilities conducive for learning and is very accessible. We offer holistic quality education and talent development for all our learners.

MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR

It is a great honor for me to address you on behalf of Eden primary schools. We are focused on preparing children for the changing conditions of today’s and tomorrow’s worlds. If we want our children to take part in the competition of the 21st century, and take the lead, they should have such qualities as self-confidence, assertiveness, leadership and all other values we stand for. As parents, our utmost responsibility is to provide an educational environment for our children for classwork and talent development. Simply offering a competitive curriculum is not enough, thus, only with well-developed interpersonal and social skills and a sense of ethics will our children truly be equipped for the challenges they will face as the leaders of the future. With that goal in mind, welcome to Eden primary schools.

MESSAGE FROM THE HEADTEACHER
